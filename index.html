Our Fleet

At WAV, we are dedicated to providing top-quality wheelchair accessible vehicles with side or rear entry, 4-point tie-down systems, seat belts, and Q'Lock or EZ Lock options. Our self-drive, automatic vehicles can accommodate up to 4-5 additional passengers and are available from leading manufacturers like Dodge, Toyota, and Chevrolet from 2017-2021. Featuring lowered floors and electric or manual ramps with a kneeling system, our vehicles offer easy access and comfort. We also offer ramps from trusted manufacturers such as Braun, VMI, Adaptive Vans, and ATS for added reliability. Let us help you find the perfect vehicle for your transport needs and give you the freedom to take on everyday challenges.
